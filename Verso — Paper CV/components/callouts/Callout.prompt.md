**Callout** — set a note, recommendation, or pull-quote apart from the running text. Use sparingly: one per section at most.

```jsx
<Callout variant="note" title="Availability" icon="calendar-blank">
  Taking on two private commissions for autumn 2026.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="quote" cite="The Fine Press Book Association">
  The most exacting compositor working in Britain today.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="aside">A marginal note in the quiet sunken panel.</Callout>
```

`note` is a raised bordered panel; `quote` is a display-serif pull-quote with a hanging mark and `cite`; `aside` is a sunken panel with an accent edge. Icons are Phosphor glyph names.
